About us!


We are relatilers. When thinking of sustainable and ethical fast fashion, Lious Fash is the first name that comes to mind. Since the launch of the fashion house in 2014, it is one of the pioneers of eco-friendly fashion and offers sustainable wardrobe alternatives.

Today, Lious Fash spans women’s ready to wear, menswear, kids’, maternity clothing, sports items, sports clothing , gift items and recycling products. The materials used by us include organic cotton, ethically sourced wool, recycled textiles while excluding fur and leather.

Our collections are now sold in multiple countries via our online platform.
